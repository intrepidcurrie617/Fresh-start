Reentry-Friendly Banks

Chime Bank - No ChexSystems check, mobile-first banking

LendingClub - Second chance banking programs

GoBank - Accepts alternative ID forms

Wells Fargo Opportunity Checking - Designed for those with banking history issues

Tips: Call ahead to ask about their second chance banking programs. Bring multiple forms of ID and be honest about your situation.

Credit Building Guide

Step 1: Get a secured credit card (requires deposit)

Step 2: Use only 10-30% of available credit

Step 3: Pay bills on time, every time

Step 4: Monitor your credit report regularly

Step 5: Be patient - building credit takes time

Recommended Secured Cards: Capital One Secured, Discover it Secured, OpenSky Secured
